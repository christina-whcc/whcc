Our Story

A Legacy of Grace on Wheels

Since 2008, White Horse & Carriage Co. has brought the romance of horse-drawn tradition to life across Texas. From grand wedding entrances in San Antonio to solemn funeral processions in Houston, we have served over 500 events with impeccably groomed horses and beautifully maintained carriages — handled with professionalism and care.

We understand that every event is unique. Our team works closely with you to ensure your carriage experience is perfectly tailored — from floral draping to route planning.
