Common Questions

Houston FAQs

What horse-drawn carriage services do you offer in Houston?
We offer a full range of horse-drawn carriage and equestrian services throughout Greater Houston. For weddings, we provide elegant carriage arrivals and grand exits — including our iconic Cinderella carriage. For South Asian celebrations, we offer a fully costumed baraat horse for the groom to ride, plus carriage rides for family and guests. We also provide horse-drawn hearse and riderless horse services for funeral processions, festive carriages for quinceañeras and parades, and custom arrangements for corporate events, photo shoots, proposals, and any special occasion.
What do your baraat services include in Houston?
Our Houston baraat package includes a beautifully decorated horse for the groom to ride in the procession, as well as a horse-drawn carriage available for family members and guests to ride alongside. Our baraat horse wears a full, authentic Indian ceremonial costume and is specially trained for the energy of these events — completely at ease with loud music, dhol drumming, dancing crowds, and the excitement of a traditional South Asian wedding procession. We serve Hindu, Sikh, Muslim, and all South Asian and Middle Eastern wedding traditions.
How far in advance should I book my Houston event?
The sooner the better! Our Houston calendar fills up quickly, especially in December and April which are our busiest months. Even if you're not quite ready to officially book, we encourage you to reach out early and let us know you're interested. That way we can keep you informed of any changes in availability for your date and make sure you don't miss out.
Do you offer horse-drawn hearse and funeral services in Houston?
Yes. We provide dignified horse-drawn hearse services throughout Houston and Harris County, coordinating directly with funeral homes and families to ensure a seamless and honorable procession. We also offer a riderless horse — a deeply meaningful military and ceremonial tribute — for those who wish to honor a loved one in that tradition. Every detail is handled with the utmost care and respect.
Can I customize the carriage decorations for my event?
Absolutely. We work with you to tailor the look of the carriage and horse to match your event's theme, colors, and vision. Options include floral draping, ribbon and fabric accents, custom plume colors, and ceremonial adornments. Whether you want a classic white romantic aesthetic or something bold and personalized, we'll make sure every detail reflects your style.
How many people can ride in the carriage?
Our traditional and Cinderella-style carriages comfortably accommodate 4–6 passengers. For larger groups, we also have bigger vehicles available, and we can bring multiple carriages to your event as needed. Every celebration is different, and we love working through the details with you to find the perfect arrangement. Reach out to us and we'll put together a plan that fits your vision and guest count perfectly.
What happens if it rains on the day of my event?
Houston weather can be unpredictable, and we plan for it. We monitor conditions closely leading up to your event and communicate with you in advance. Light rain generally doesn't stop us — our horses are experienced working in varied conditions. For heavier weather, we'll work with you on timing adjustments or contingency plans to make sure your experience is as magical as possible regardless of the forecast.
What areas of Greater Houston do you serve?
We serve all of Greater Houston and surrounding South Texas communities including The Woodlands, Sugar Land, Katy, Pearland, Friendswood, League City, Pasadena, Missouri City, Conroe, Sealy, Columbus, Seguin, San Marcos, and beyond. If you're unsure whether your venue falls within our service area, just reach out — we're happy to discuss logistics for your specific location.
