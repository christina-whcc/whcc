Common Questions

Austin FAQs

What horse-drawn carriage services do you offer in Austin?
We offer a full range of horse-drawn carriage and equestrian services throughout Austin and Central Texas. For weddings, we provide elegant carriage arrivals and grand exits — including our iconic Cinderella carriage, perfect for The Arlo and other premier Austin venues. For South Asian celebrations, we offer a fully costumed baraat horse for the groom to ride, plus carriage rides for family and guests. We also provide horse-drawn hearse and riderless horse services for funeral processions, festive carriages for quinceañeras, Hill Country wedding arrivals, photo shoots, corporate events, proposals, and any special occasion across Greater Austin.
What do your baraat services include in Austin?
Our Austin baraat package includes a beautifully decorated horse in a full authentic Indian ceremonial costume for the groom to ride, as well as a horse-drawn carriage available for family members and guests to ride alongside. Our baraat horse is specially trained for the energy of these celebrations — completely at ease with loud music, dhol drumming, dancing crowds, and the excitement of a traditional South Asian wedding procession. We serve Hindu, Sikh, Muslim, and all South Asian and Middle Eastern wedding traditions throughout Austin and Central Texas.
How far in advance should I book my Austin event?
The sooner the better! Austin's event calendar fills up fast, especially in December and April which are our busiest months. Hill Country wedding venues in particular tend to book out well in advance. Even if you're not quite ready to officially book, reach out early and let us know you're interested — we'll keep you informed of any changes in availability for your date so you don't miss out.
Do you offer horse-drawn hearse and funeral services in Austin?
Yes. We provide dignified horse-drawn hearse services throughout Austin and the surrounding Central Texas area, including Evergreen Cemetery and funeral homes across Travis, Williamson, and Hays counties. We also offer a riderless horse — a deeply meaningful military and ceremonial tribute. We coordinate directly with funeral homes and families to ensure every detail is handled with the utmost care and respect.
Do you serve Hill Country wedding venues near Austin?
Absolutely — Hill Country weddings are one of our specialties. We regularly serve venues in Dripping Springs, Wimberley, Marble Falls, the Texas Wine Country corridor, and throughout the scenic ranch and vineyard venues surrounding Austin. Our horses are perfectly suited to outdoor rustic settings, and we handle all route planning and logistics so your experience is seamless from start to finish.
Can I customize the carriage decorations for my Austin event?
Absolutely. We work with you to tailor the look of the carriage and horse to match your event's theme, colors, and vision. Options include floral draping, ribbon and fabric accents, custom plume colors, and ceremonial adornments. Whether you want a classic white romantic aesthetic for a Hill Country vineyard wedding or something bold and personalized for a quinceañera, we'll make sure every detail reflects your style.
How many people can ride in the carriage?
Our traditional and Cinderella-style carriages comfortably accommodate 4–6 passengers. For larger groups, we also have bigger vehicles available, and we can bring multiple carriages to your event as needed. Every celebration is different, and we love working through the details with you to find the perfect arrangement. Reach out to us and we'll put together a plan that fits your vision and guest count perfectly.
What happens if it rains on the day of my event?
Austin weather can change quickly, and we plan for it. We monitor conditions closely in the days leading up to your event and stay in communication with you throughout. Light rain generally doesn't stop us — our horses are experienced working in varied conditions. For heavier weather, we'll work with you on timing adjustments or contingency plans to make sure your experience is as magical as possible regardless of the forecast.
What areas of Central Texas do you serve?
We serve all of Greater Austin and the full I-35 corridor through Central Texas — including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Buda, Kyle, San Marcos, New Braunfels, Seguin, Dripping Springs, Wimberley, Marble Falls, Bastrop, Lockhart, and all surrounding South and Central Texas communities. If you're unsure whether your venue is in our service area, just reach out — we're happy to discuss logistics for your specific location.
